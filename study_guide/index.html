TM (Tecnologías Multimedia) is an optative subject of the Computer Science Degree at the UAL (University of Almería).

1 Methodology

TM follows the PBL (Project-Based Learning) methodology. The students, helped by the teacher, develop a project during the classes. This project is InterCom(municator), an application that allows networked users to communicate throught the Internet.

The project is implemented as a sequence of milestones. The students, in groups of up to 4 people, propose solutions for each milestone. Such solutions are presented to the rest of the class, and each group gives and receives a score and feedback from the rest of the class and also from the teacher.

2 Milestones

  1. Week 1: About the framework.

    1. OS provisioning.

    2. Basic programming with Python.

    3. Git, GitHub and the Fork-and-Branch Git Workﬂow.

  2. Week 2: The simplest InterCom.

    1. ‘Wiring” the ADC with the DAC and measuring latencies.

    2. Minimal InterCom.

  3. Week 3: Dealing with transmission latencies and bit-rates.

    1. Impact of the jitter.

    2. Impact of the bit-rate.

    3. Buﬀering.

  4. Week 4: Compressing and quantizing.

    1. Compressing the audio data with zlib.

    2. Bit-rate control through quantization.

  5. Week 5: Removing temporal and spatial redundancy with transform coding.

    1. Stereo coding of audio signals.

    2. Temporal coding of audio signals.

    3. Overlapped DWT.

  6. Week 6: Removing psychoacousting redundancy.

    1. Considering the threshold of hearing.

    2. (TODO) Considering the simultaneous masking eﬀect.
